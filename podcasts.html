What's this all about?

We are Paul Smid and Mark Tensen, two Amsterdam locals in our twenties. The QuarterLifeCrisis podcast is our little platonic love baby. In each episode we'll explore topics like spirituality, self-development, philosophy and everything else that we find interesting and important. Although we are both self-development junkies we are by no means experts in any of these matters. Thus the style of the QuarterLifeCrisis podcast is very exploratory.
In a light manner we'll discuss the value, paradoxes and mysteries of our topics and thereby hopefully spark your enthusiasm on these matters too. Also, helped by the occasional guest we will try to give you a fresh viewpoint on that what we deem important in our rapidly changing world. That’s all for now…. See you in the next episode!
